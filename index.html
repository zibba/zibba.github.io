Abuse Report System

×

Abuse report management system is for health care organization to prevent fake accusations of abuse against staff members. It developed complex MVC Controller, Views and Models to implement the work flow of the abuse report system using Java and Play. The purpose is because of patients' disabilities, they can be hard to work with. They may become violent and need to be restrained at times. The restraints if not properly, it can injure the client. In some situations, patients make false accusations of abuse against staff members. Any of these situations result in an abuse report which must be investigated and resolved.

Doc Management System

×

Defying Disaster

×

Defying Disaster is a serious game with the features of an adventure game, aiming to teach people professional knowledge and training skills about preparing for and dealing with disasters in an immersive and interactive environment. I am currently in contact with disaster preparedness experts at the Federal Emergency Management Association (FEMA).

Jump Singing Ball

×

Singing Ball is VR game, allows users to create and save melody with LeapMotion – a somatic input device can track hands in real world. Move hands to hit different notes (Do, Re, etc) to create melody. Developed with Unity3D, Photoshop (Software), MiddleVR (Middleware) and LeapMotion (Hardware).

Lost Island

×

Lost island is a 3D puzzle game, which developed with Unreal Engineer 4. For ten years, many ships sink in this area around the island, then survivals will drift to the same island by the wind and trap in this island. This island just have one locked house, a light house, and a boat on the top of light house. No one here know how to get this boat for escaping from the island. Finally, they will died in this island, people always call it lost island. Based on the clues in the environment, player should find a way to escape from the island.
